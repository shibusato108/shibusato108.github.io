Recent Business News in Higashi-Matsuyama

Recently, automobile industry are facing challenging because of shifting to electric automobile. Additionally, pandemic of COVID19 causes sales of automobile decline. Therefore, 40% of factory are inactive and sales are declining seriously. Therefore, the chamber of commerce presided a meeting of auto industry to prepare for the future. For details for the meeting, magazine sent to those who are members.

For restaurant industry also has serious inpact because of COVID19 pandemic. Average of Sales is about 30% compated to 2019. Many of the business submitted permission to open restaurant after 9:00pm which against the rule of lockdown. However, govenment said there would be fine to those who open restaurant after 9:00 pm regardless of permission. Restaurant industry required to change the business model. For example, shift to delivery, focus more on lunch time and so on.
