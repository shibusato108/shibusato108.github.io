Who this site is for

For those who are studying online from several scholls in the world. For these days, Online learning is available in many places, however, when people meet online they need to consider time difference and good to know about each locations. So, for those who are learning, not only learn about the subject, but to virtually visit each location which will make learning experience better.

Senarios

  1. How can I enjoy many location without traveling.
  2. I want to travel, but not able becaue of pandemic. How can I have feeling of visiting.
  3. I want to study about many locations, what is each locations difference.
  4. How can I understand about diversity.
  5. How can I find California or Hawaii's time while I'm in Tokyo.
